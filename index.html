About DLE

A Dual Language program is a teaching model designed to promote bilingualism and biliteracy, cross-cultural competency, and high levels of academic achievement for both native English speakers and ELs from a single language background, Travis Primary focuses in teaching students both in Spanish and English.


Best Practices

At Travis, we follow the Gomez & Gomez academic best practices daily which are wide range of individual and partner activities, policies, and programmatic approaches to achieve positive changes in student attitudes or academic behaviors when learning that second language.
